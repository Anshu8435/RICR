Lists in HTML are a powerful way to organize information in a clear and structured format. They help readers quickly scan and understand important points without reading long paragraphs. Ordered lists display items in a numbered sequence, making them useful for step-by-step instructions. Unordered lists use bullet points, which are perfect for highlighting collections of related ideas. By combining lists with proper formatting, web designers can improve readability and user experience. Overall, lists transform plain text into organized, meaningful, and visually appealing content that enhances communication on any webpage.
